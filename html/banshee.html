Banshee Boss Hunt

Banshee Crafting Sets:

Destruction Set
Lifesteal Set
Counter Set
Effect Resist

Banshee is significantly more difficult to do than wyvern but is necessary for players to correctly equip gear on certain characters. While the sets that you can craft are not super commonly used, the characters that use them utilize them very well.

There are two main strategies to clearing the Banshee hunt. The first is the one shot method. The goal of this one is to kill the boss in a single hit so that it is unable to split into the smaller banshees. This speeds up the time of a clear significantly but is difficult to achieve.

The second method is needed for when people are unable to kill the boss in a single hit. You will need to clear the five banshees that split from the main boss as fast as possible to avoid dying and to speed up your clear time. Thankfully, these banshee spawns are not very strong and you often will only need one area of effect (AoE) character to clear them out.
