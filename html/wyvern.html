Wyvern is arguably the best hunt to run of the three main hunts. Speed is a very important stat for characters to have in most any game, which wyvern provides. Clearing Wyvern Stage 11 on autobattle is a very good goal for a mid-level account to achieve. This will provide the player with good access to equipment and gold generation that will help them create units that will be usable in PvP content.

Wyvern Stage 11 has many benefits over the other wyvern stages. It only drops level 70-85 gear and the highest level of crafting material for its sets. The boss also only attacks the unit that you have in the front of your party, making your non-frontline tank units able to focus more on damage. It is also significantly easier than Wyvern Stage 13 and provides the same amount of crafting material.
